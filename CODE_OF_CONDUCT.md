# Code of Conduct

We want a friendly, inclusive community. Be respectful and assume good intent.

## Expected behavior
- Be kind and constructive.
- Focus on the problem, not the person.
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences.

## Unacceptable behavior
- Harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks.
- Disruptive or abusive conduct.
- Sharing private information without consent.

## Scope
This applies to all project spaces: issues, PRs, discussions, and community channels.

## Enforcement
Maintainers may remove content or restrict access to keep the community safe.
Report issues using GitHub's built-in reporting tools.
